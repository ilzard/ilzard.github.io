Description

NIX UMBRA is an endless slow-burning first-person horror ritual set in the enchanted forest. How long can you survive the darkness?

Features

  • Unique psychedelic monochrome aesthetic. Collect solar crystals and unlock new spectral colors.
  • Random generated wood & terror for maximal replayability. (no scripted jumpscares)
  • Global leaderboard. Transcend yourself, reach the astral realm!
  • 8 Enemy types including fire ghosts, inverse kinematic spiders and flying witches.
  • 11 magical Power-ups such as shape-altering mushrooms, frog and fairy.
  • Occult game mechanics. Out-of-body experience, tree healing and rose gazing gameplay.
